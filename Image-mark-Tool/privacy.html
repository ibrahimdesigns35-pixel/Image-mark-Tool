Privacy Policy

Transparency and security are at the heart of our service.

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Image Mark Tool ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure environment for all our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and watermarking tool.

2. The "No-Upload" Guarantee

The most important part of our privacy commitment is our technical architecture. Unlike many online image tools, **Image Mark Tool processes all images locally on your device**.

  • No Image Storage: Your images are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Client-Side Processing: All watermarking and manipulation happen within your browser's memory using HTML5 Canvas technology.
  • Total Control: Once you close your browser tab, any temporary data used for the watermarking process is instantly cleared from your device's memory.

3. Information We Collect

Since the core functionality of our tool is client-side, we collect very minimal information:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytical data (such as page views and feature usage) to help us improve the tool. This does not include any of your images or personal identifiers.
  • Contact Information: If you voluntarily reach out to us via our contact form, we collect your name and email address to respond to your inquiry.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use a limited number of cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality.
  • Analytics: Temporary cookies that help us understand site performance. You can disable these via your browser settings.

5. Data Protection

Because we do not store your images, there is no risk of your visual data being leaked from our servers. For the minimal contact data we might hold, we implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party fonts (Google Fonts) and content delivery networks (CDNs) to serve site assets efficiently. These services may collect basic connection data (like your IP address) as part of their standard operation.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal info, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
